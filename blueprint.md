
# Blueprint for "Rumah Tropis" Website

## 1. Overview

*   **Purpose:** To create a visually stunning and highly engaging website for "Rumah Tropis" that showcases the beauty and elegance of tropical living.
*   **Target Audience:** Individuals and families interested in modern, tropical-inspired homes.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   A beautiful and intuitive user interface.
    *   Mobile-responsive design.
    *   Engaging content and visuals.
    *   Clear and easy-to-use navigation.

## 2. Design and Style

*   **Aesthetics:** Modern, clean, and elegant, with a focus on natural elements and a sense of tranquility.
*   **Color Palette:** A sophisticated mix of earthy tones, vibrant accent colors, and plenty of white space.
*   **Typography:** Expressive and easy-to-read fonts that create a clear visual hierarchy.
*   **Iconography & Imagery:** High-quality images and icons that are both beautiful and meaningful.
*   **Interactivity:** Subtle animations and "glow" effects on interactive elements to create a sense of depth and engagement.

## 3. Feature Implementation Plan

*   **Initial Setup:** Create the foundational layout and styling for the entire application.
*   **Homepage:** Design a captivating hero section with a compelling headline, a brief introduction, and a clear call-to-action.
*   **Navigation:** Implement a user-friendly navigation bar.
*   **Footer:** Add a comprehensive footer with important links and contact information.
